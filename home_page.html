About Student Affairs

Purpose and goals

  • Increasing student engagement and involvement in campus life
  • Improving student retention and success
  • Providing access to support services and resources for students
  • Promoting campus events and activities
  • Enhancing communication and collaboration among stakeholders

Features and functionality

  • Navigation menus to help users find information and resources
  • Calendars of events and activities happening on campus
  • Forms and applications for various services and programs
  • Directories of campus offices and staff members
  • Information about campus policies and procedures
  • Resources for academic and personal support,
  • such as tutoring and counseling services
  • Student organization directories and resources
  • News and announcements about campus events and initiatives
  • Social media integration to connect with students and stakeholders
